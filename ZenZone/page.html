Online Chat Sessions

Online chat sessions for therapy, also called e-therapy, are virtual one-on-one conversations between a therapist and a client. These sessions use text-based chat platforms for discussing emotions, challenges, and personal growth, providing support and guidance similar to traditional face-to-face therapy.

One-to-one Call Sessions

1:1 therapy, known as counseling or psychotherapy, is a one-on-one session between a trained therapist and a client to address emotional issues, improve coping skills, and enhance personal growth.

Behavioral Theory

It's a psychological approach that focuses on how behaviors are learned and can be modified through conditioning. It emphasizes observable behaviors and the role of the environment in shaping and reinforcing actions, often using techniques like rewards and punishments to encourage desired behaviors.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy(CBT)

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, is a form of psychotherapy that helps individuals by focusing on changing negative thought patterns and behaviors. It aims to improve emotional regulation and coping skills by identifying and challenging unhelpful beliefs and developing healthier ways of thinking and behaving.

24/7 Support

24/7 support assistance and help available at any time, day or night, every day of the week. This support is continuous and immediate, often used in contexts such as crisis hotlines or online services for mental health, where individuals can receive help whenever they need it.

Multilingual Support

Multilingual support offers services or assistance in multiple languages, allowing individuals who speak different languages to access and benefit from the same resources or services.
